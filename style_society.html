About the project:

A web service designed to promote sustainable consumption by offering rentals of designer clothing. The project aims to reduce unnecessary consumption and encourage a sustainable lifestyle by providing a platform where people can rent designer clothing for special occasions and rent out clothing they no longer wear. This was created as part of an application for the Digital Designer program at Yrgo.
